Courses
BTECH

JB Knowledge Park in Manjhawali offers a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program that provides students with a solid foundation in various engineering disciplines. With a focus on innovative learning and hands-on experience, the institution emphasizes the development of technical skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities. The campus is equipped with modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry-aligned curricula, which prepare students for successful careers in engineering. JB Knowledge Park fosters a vibrant academic environment that encourages research, creativity, and professional growth among its students.

BCA

JB Knowledge Park, located in Manjhawali, offers a comprehensive Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and information technology. The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including programming languages, database management, networking, web development, and software engineering. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and hands-on learning opportunities, JB Knowledge Park ensures that students are well-equipped to meet the demands of the IT industry, preparing them for successful careers in the digital age.

BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at JB Knowledge Park, located in Manjhawali, is designed to equip students with a strong foundation in business principles and management practices. The course focuses on providing a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills in areas such as finance, marketing, human resources, and entrepreneurship. With modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a dynamic learning environment, JB Knowledge Park ensures students are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the corporate world and succeed in their professional careers.
